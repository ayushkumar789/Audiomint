FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What links are supported?

Spotify track, playlist, album, or artist URLs. Paste one or many (one per line is fine).

Which audio formats do you offer?

MP3, M4A/AAC, Opus, and FLAC. MP3 is the most compatible; FLAC is lossless.

What happens with playlists and albums?

We return a ZIP containing all tracks. Keep the tab open while we prepare your files.

Do you embed tags and cover art?

Yes—title, artists, album, track number, year, and cover art are embedded automatically.

Can I paste multiple Spotify links at once?

Yep—paste one per line. We’ll queue them and deliver a single ZIP if there’s more than one track.

The “Preparing your download…” screen doesn’t finish

This usually means YouTube/YouTube Music throttled the fetch. Try again, or switch the format to MP3/M4A and keep the tab open. If it persists, try a different browser.

Why do cookies or a YouTube login help?

Authenticated sessions reduce rate-limits and improve match quality for some tracks.

Is this legal? Can I use downloads commercially?

Personal/educational use only. Make sure you have the right to download the audio you request.

Do you store my links or files?

No. Temporary job folders are cleaned automatically; we don’t keep your audio or URLs.

Something failed. What should I try next?

Try MP3 first, then M4A. If a track stalls, retry or change browsers. Still stuck? Drop us a note on the Contact page with the Spotify link you tried.
